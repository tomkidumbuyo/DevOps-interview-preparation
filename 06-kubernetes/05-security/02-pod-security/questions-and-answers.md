Compare them with source-controlled intent and a known healthy baseline, and correlate the nearest event, metric, log, trace or audit record. **Failure and trade-off.** Empty output, permission denial, stale state and an unavailable dependency require different next checks; immediately mutating the target destroys evidence and may widen impact. **Production example.** During a Pod and node workload security rollout, an operator uses this check to decide whether the fault is in accepted configuration, reconciliation or the live data path, and verifies the final repair from the original client.
