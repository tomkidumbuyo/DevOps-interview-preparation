Recover in waves so error, latency, correctness, security and billing evidence can halt expansion. **Close the loop.** Verify GCP operations, security and cost from the user path, reconcile the durable source, publish the timeline and trade-off record or postmortem, assign preventive actions with owners and dates, and prove them in a restore test, game day or migration rehearsal.

## Self-grading

A complete spoken answer names the mechanism, scope/assumptions, one concrete command or configuration field, a failure mode, security/recovery, an SLI and a cost/trade-off. For procedural answers, explicitly separate immediate reversible mitigation from durable source-of-truth repair.
